## 2. The Problem
-   **The Silent Crisis:** Prolonged sitting and screen time lead to "Tech Neck," chronic back pain, and Computer Vision Syndrome.
-   **The Focus Trap:** We get so absorbed in work that we forget to blink or sit up straight until it hurts.
-   **The Privacy Concern:** Existing solutions often require cloud processing, raising concerns about having a camera pointed at you all day.

## 4. How It Works
1.  **Install & Launch:** Download the lightweight app for macOS.
2.  **Calibrate:** Sit comfortably and let ErgoSense learn your neutral spine position.
3.  **Work Normally:** The app runs in the background, analyzing video frames locally.
4.  **Get Notified:** Receive non-intrusive alerts when you need to adjust your posture or take a break.
5.  **Review Progress:** Check your dashboard at the end of the day to see your stats.

## 5. Technical Specifications
-   **Platform:** macOS (Windows & Linux coming soon)

-   **AI Engine:** TensorFlow / MediaPipe (Optimized for CPU/GPU)
